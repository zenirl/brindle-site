Questions

Does my sitter need to install anything?

No. You send a link; they open it in any phone browser. They can mark feedings done, log walks, and send you a photo — no app, no account.

Is my data private?

Your pet records live on your phone. Sharing is opt-in: only when you tap “Share with sitter” does a one-time snapshot go online, tied to a random code that expires (and that you can revoke any time). See the privacy policy.

Can I use it for more than one pet?

Yes — unlimited pets, no per-pet fee, ever.

Is the vaccination proof good enough for a kennel or daycare?

It lists each shot with the date given and next-due date, exportable as a focused PDF. Many boarders accept owner-provided records; check with yours.
