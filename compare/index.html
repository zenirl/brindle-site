Brindle vs 11pets: built for the handoff.

11pets is a deep, all-in-one pet manager. Brindle is deliberately narrower: it nails the one stressful moment — handing your pet to a sitter, kennel, or daycare — and prices it like software you own, not rent.

When Brindle is the better fit

If your main need is the going-away handoff — not a full clinical record system — the simpler tool wins.

✈️

You travel or board often

Trip mode, sitter link, and vaccination proof are the whole point — not a buried sub-feature.

💸

You hate subscriptions

Pay once. Keeping your own pet's records shouldn't be a monthly bill.

🔒

You want your data local

Records stay on your phone; sharing is opt-in and revocable.
