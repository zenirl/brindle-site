# Pet Log

A simple, local-first pet health & care tracker for Android.

- **Pets**: vaccinations, vet visits, weight, medications
- **Routines**: feeding schedule, walk log, inventory
- **Reminders**: notifications when shots / meds are due
- **Sitter mode**: share a one-tap web link with whoever's watching your pet
- **No accounts, no ads, no tracking** — data stays on your phone
